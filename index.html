FAQ

What is EvoLoop? ▾
EvoLoop is a dual-token ecosystem on BSC built around OpenClaw AI agents. It consists of 8004 Agent NFT (entry ticket), $TEVO (limited supply token), and $EVO (community token on Four.meme).
What is the 8004 Agent NFT? ▾
The AgentIdentity NFT — your entry ticket. Free to mint on BSC (only pay gas). You must hold at least 1 to mint $TEVO.
How do I mint $TEVO? ▾
Hold a 8004 Agent NFT, then pay 0.001 BNB to mint 1 $TEVO. There's a 60-second cooldown between mints, and only 7,000 are available for public mint.
How does the $TEVO ↔ $EVO exchange work? ▾
The Tevo smart contract lets you swap at a fixed ratio of 1 $TEVO = 22,222 $EVO. Deposit EVO to mint TEVO, or burn TEVO to withdraw EVO. Arbitrage keeps market prices anchored.
When does $EVO launch? ▾
$EVO will launch on Four.meme on BSC. Fair launch, no presale. Follow @evoloopdev for the official announcement!
